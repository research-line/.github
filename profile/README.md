# research-line

**Open-access research publications and computational science tools.**

research-line hosts reproducible research projects spanning physics, mathematics, cosmology, AI sociology, and clinical psychology. All publications are available as open access via [Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/) with persistent DOIs.

| [crm-cosmology](https://github.com/research-line/crm-cosmology) | Cosmology | Conformal Rescaling Model for dark energy |
